# FamilyOps

AI family chief-of-staff. Forward (or connect) the email your family already
gets — school, doctor, activities — and get **one trustworthy daily brief**
across both parents and all your kids.

## Why it can win where Google won't
- **Cross-person, cross-provider household graph** — the same obligation
  arriving in mom's Gmail *and* dad's Outlook collapses to one assigned
  to-do. Google's single-account model has no place to put this.
- **Rent the engine, own the graph** — extraction runs on `agent-chassis`
  (Claude/Gemini, swappable). The graph, dedup, assignment and brief are ours.
- **Data you control** — premium "Vault" tier stores in the user's own
  GCP/AWS/Azure. A pitch the ad-funded incumbents structurally can't make.

## Beachhead
Education-ops (school logistics) — weakest incumbent, strongest AI advantage
(it's all unstructured email/PDF). Expand: Education → Health → Finance.
